Ryu
The original shoto character, Ryu embodies all the meaningful aspects of Street Fighter gameplay in one concise package. Ryu emphasizes a patient playstyle with strong defensive tools using his signature moves: Hadoken and Shoryuken.
Luke
Luke plays similarly to Ryu, but with a stronger emphasis on midrange pressure and whiff punishing.
